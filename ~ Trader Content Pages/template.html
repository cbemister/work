RAM 3500

When your work demands the toughest of trucks, the Ram 3500 Heavy Duty more than steps up to the plate. Boasting huge power under the hood combined with luxurious comfort in the cabin, it's a full-size truck that delivers on every front. There are two main engine options available in the latest lineup. The range starts with a 6.4L V8 gas model making an impressive 410 horsepower and 429lb-ft of torque, passed to either rear-wheel or all-wheel drive through an eight-speed automatic transmission. And if you need even more load-hauling performance, switching to the high-power 6.7L turbodiesel mill raises the horsepower to 420 hp and cranks the torque up to a mammoth 1,075lb-ft, a class-leading output that turns the Ram 3500 into a real workhorse.

Learn more about the RAM 3500

All this advanced engineering delivers some deeply impressive capability figures, with a payload rating of up to 3,491kg and an unrivalled maximum towing capacity of 11,743kg. But the Ram 3500's outstanding qualities don't end there, as the spacious, comfortable interior is packed with the latest technology. Highlights include an optional 12" infotainment touchscreen giving access to navigation, smartphone integration, a wi-fi hotspot facility, active safety systems, and much more.
